## Problem

Find a matrix $$\mathbf{B} \neq \mathbf{I}$$ in $$\mathbf{M}_{2}(\mathbb{R})$$ such that $$\mathbf{B}^3 = \mathbf{I}$$

## Solution
Choose $$\mathbf{B}$$ as below, then you can check it.

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
-1 & -1\\
1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
$$
